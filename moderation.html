These rules apply to Discord and community spaces. Stay respectful and keep it fair.

Be open-minded and respectful. Keep the server family-friendly and avoid drama or sensitive topics.

  • No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or toxic behavior.
  • No rolecism (discrimination based on roles or role colors).
  • No ragebaiting or instigating conflicts.
2

Username & Profile Standards

Choose your username and profile details carefully.

  • Inappropriate names or avatars may result in removal without notice.
  • Suspicious guild tags or compromised-looking accounts may be removed.
3

  • No NSFW, sexual, or inappropriate content.

BAN APPEALS

How to Appeal a Ban

If you believe your ban was issued in error or you'd like to appeal after serving your punishment, follow these steps:

1

Wait Period

Temporary bans: Appeal after ban expires
Permanent bans: Wait minimum 30 days

2

Gather Information

Your username, ban date, reason for ban, and any evidence supporting your appeal

3

Submit Appeal

Contact through communication server or message a staff member on Roblox

4

Be Patient

Appeals are reviewed within 3-7 days. Do not spam or harass staff for updates
